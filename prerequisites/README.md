# PyTorch Zero to Hero: The Foundations

Welcome to the start of your deep learning journey! This repository is designed to take you from a complete beginner to building complex neural networks, one step at a time.

We believe in **learning by doing**, so every chapter is a self-contained Jupyter notebook that introduces one core concept without overwhelming you.

## Currently Released Chapters

We are releasing this course progressively. Here are the first two "atoms" of the deep learning universe:

### [Chapter 2: Autograd - The Engine of Learning](./02_Autograd.ipynb)

*   **Analogy:** A magical assistant that follows you around and records every math operation you do, so it can replay it backwards later.
